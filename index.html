Projects
The project model of training is the solution of urgent tasks from industrial partners by MIEM student teams

Working in the format of project implementation for the tasks of state and business structures is one of the most effective methods of training personnel in higher education. Actual tasks from enterprises of the real sector of the economy, interdisciplinarity, teamwork skills and, most importantly, specific and in-demand results of such work-all this makes the project model of training in demand for students, businesses and educational organizations.

The design and technological model of training as a mandatory element of the general educational process for students of the 3rd year of bachelor's degree was introduced at MIEM in the 2018-2019 academic year. Currently, the project work includes students of 2-3 bachelor's courses and all master's students.

At the end of each academic year, all projects are subject to expert control in the form of defenses, where the most interesting and successful projects are identified based on their results, which will be presented as part of a single exhibition event - a Technoshow, where guests will be able to visit the project offices of the teams, ask questions, discuss the presented area of information technology with the guys. Also, a live broadcast of the main stage of the Technoshow is planned throughout the event, which will introduce the audience to the most interesting developments of MIEM students.
